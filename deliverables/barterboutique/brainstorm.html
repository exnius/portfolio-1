Research: Brainstorming


We brainstormed around the four service areas to help us identify which service was the most interesting space. We created affinity diagrams of each area and came up with our own personal ideas of what we could explore in the space. Next, we each designated three votes to the top ideas. Our final choices included personal health and boutique retail. We decided to find a solution in boutique retail because we felt there was more opportunity to innovate in this space.
