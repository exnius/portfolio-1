Research

To create a novel and innovative bartering experience, we had to consider our users' needs. We began by exploring user needs using directed storytelling, performing a competitive evaluation of existing barter websites, and by performing brick and mortar observations. We prototyped a barter site based on the limitations we found from our competitive analysis and the opportunities we identified from our directed storytelling and brick and mortar observation results. We completed our process by contributing a feature set to create the best possible barter experience.

To see how we derived upon bartering as our main idea, refer to the Brainstorm section of our research page.
