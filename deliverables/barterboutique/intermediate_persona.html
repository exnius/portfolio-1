Sarah Hofstadter, 35, Pittsburgh, PA

Bio

Sara is a 35 year old female from Pittsburgh, PA. She works at the Phantom of the Attic comic book shop with her husband in Oakland. She has worked there for three years and has been a hard-core collector of comic books for three years(Marvel only). Sara goes to Comic Cons every year and tries to trade for rare comics. She knows when someone is trying to cheat her.

Goals

Sara's goal is to build a strong community among comic book traders so that they can all look out for each other and prevent each other from bad deals. Her other goals including filling the gaps in her Daredevil collection (issues 50-103). She has used both online sites like eBay and attends cons, visits flea markets and of course, comic stores.
