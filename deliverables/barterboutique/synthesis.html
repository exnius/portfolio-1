Synthesis

Our design direction as shown in our mood boards gives a feeling of rarity, fun, and and pride. The colors within each image give a feeling of warmth and trust. We planned to have a simple, direct, and clear interface without overwhelming amount of text and visuals.

From our user research, we identified the following opportunities around bartering and trading:
  • Need for organization (particularly in pawn shops and online bartering sites such as Craigslist and bartertown.com)
  • Need for community to help establish trust and to enforce fairness
  • Profitability for everyone as a result of obtaining the maximum value from trades
  • Elimination of middleman/woman
  • Negotiation efficiency as it can be a timely process

We developed three personas that directly benefit from our site.
