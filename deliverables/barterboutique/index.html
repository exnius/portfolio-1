Barter Boutique

Intro

Barter Boutique is a website that connects a community of collectors and enables them to trade items within their collection with each other. We provide an innovative solution that increases the number of possible trades they can make with automated suggestions and chain trades. These techniques decrease the amount of effort to find a trade and increases a collector's rate of success. With Barter Boutique, collectors can complete their collections much easier when we give them the tools to trade and collect with the world.
