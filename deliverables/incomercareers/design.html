Design Process

Once we settled on the persona we hoped to address with our research, we looked at compelling scenarios. We initially thought of two:

  1. The need for potential employees to track their employment documentation of time.
  2. The need for potential international employees to get a feel for what companies are willing to hire international employees while at a job fair.

Based on these scenarios we completed an initial round of wireframes using Cacoo. The images below represent that effort. Completed by white-boarding some basic concepts then dividing out the functionality among out team members.

Based on feedback from a presentation of these screen and the research we started to conduct (found on the research page), we created an affinity of features that seemed most helpful and modified our focus to:

  1. On-the-go or pre-job fair reconnaissance about international hiring practices
  2. Job fair info gathering about international hiring practices.

The resulting work from that is our final application, which can be seen on the solution page.
