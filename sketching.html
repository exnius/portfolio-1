Sketching Highlights (pdf)

Design, especially in the world of HCI, involves the ability to communicate ideas clearly. To develop my skills in visually communicating ideas, I took a four month course at Carnegie Mellon called Methodology of Visualization. The course helped me develop abilities in deconstructing visual artifacts, perspective drawing, basic rendering, visual narratives, and storyboarding. Above all, the projects we completed have given me the ability to sketch more confidently and rapidly. In terms of both utility and enjoyment, I can easily put this class at the top of my list.
